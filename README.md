## **3. Core Features**

### **1. Agentic AI Brain**
Built using hybrid cognitive frameworks (**ACT-R** and **SOAR**) to simulate human-like problem-solving and self-regulation.  

- **ACT-R (Adaptive Control of Thought â€“ Rational):**  
  A cognitive architecture that models human cognition as modular processes â€” *declarative memory*, *procedural rules*, *goal management*, and *perceptual-motor functions*.  
  In Cognitive_AI_Advisor, ACT-R models structure how the AI **retrieves context**, **updates goals**, and **learns adaptive behaviors** based on student interactions.

- **SOAR (State, Operator, And Result):**  
  A problem-solving architecture designed for continuous reasoning and decision-making.  
  SOAR enables the system to **create subgoals**, **evaluate alternative actions**, and **learn from feedback**, making the AI capable of **strategic, multi-step reasoning**.  
  In combination with ACT-R, it allows **deliberative cognitive cycles**, helping the system understand *why* a student feels a certain way and what intervention may help best.

Together, ACT-R and SOAR form the **Cognitive Engine**, responsible for **reasoning, memory retrieval, emotional regulation, and learning from user experience**.

---

### **2. Emotion & Cognitive Understanding**
- **NLP Models:** RoBERTa, DeBERTa, and DistilBERT for intent, sentiment, and emotion recognition.  
- **Speech Analysis:** Wav2Vec2 for tone and stress detection.  
- **Multimodal Fusion:** Combines text, voice, and optional physiological data (EEG/HRV) for deep contextual understanding.

---

### **3. Recommendation Engine**
Provides holistic recommendations:
- **Music Therapy:** Personalized playlists via Spotify or YouTube APIs.  
- **Study Resources:** Context-aware suggestions based on cognitive fatigue and difficulty.  
- **Wellness Routines:** CBT-based micro-interventions for self-reflection and mindfulness.  
- **Peer Support:** Links to student support communities and mentors.  
- **Counselor Matching:** Intelligent escalation when distress patterns are detected.

---

### **4. Adaptive Personalization**
Uses:
- **Contextual Bandits** for adaptive feedback.  
- **Reinforcement Learning** to refine strategies based on user response.  
- **Continual Learning (LoRA, Replay Buffers)** to retain long-term user understanding.  

---

### **5. Safety & Transparency**
- Ensemble classifiers for **self-harm/suicide detection** using RoBERTa + rule-based signals.  
- **Explainability Tools (SHAP, Captum, CoT Prompts)** to interpret model reasoning.  
- **Ethical Escalation Pathways** to human counselors and helplines.

## **6. System Architecture**

The Cognitive_AI_Advisor pipeline integrates cognitive reasoning, perception, and action modules:

1. **Perception Layer:** Collects text, speech, and physiological signals.  
2. **Cognitive Engine:** Runs ACT-R and SOAR cycles for reasoning and decision-making.  
3. **NLP Understanding:** Extracts emotion, intent, and stress markers.  
4. **Reinforcement Loop:** Learns from feedback and adapts over time.  
5. **Safety Monitor:** Applies ethical checks and triggers support protocols.  
6. **Recommendation Engine:** Generates personalized interventions and advice.
